ToneZ

EDM Synthesizer


ToneZ is a free polyphonic synthesizer for EDM, available as VST/AU plugin for Windows and Mac OS.
With its modern looking UI, it will become one of your new best friends for supersaws, leads, basses, plucks and more !

ToneZ Features

  • 4 Oscillators
    • 12 waveforms
    • Morphing
    • 1-8 voices
    • Detune & stereo width control
  • 4 Envelopes
    • Exponential/Linear curves
    • Modulation Matrix
  • 2 Filter Units
    • Multi mode : Low pass, high pass, band pass
    • Resonance & key tracking control
  • 2 LFOs
    • 6 wave shapes
  • 6 Built-in Effects
    • Distortion
    • Equalizer
    • Chorus
    • Delay
    • Reverb
    • Compressor
  • Control Panel
    • Portamento
    • Preset system
    • Expert detune

ToneZ was made using Cabbage framework. The Csound code is available on Github.
