ToneZ [out on 03.20.19]

EDM Synthesizer


ToneZ is a free polyphonic synthesizer for EDM, available as 64-bit VST plugin.
With it's modern looking UI, it will become one of your new best friends for supersaws, leads, basses, plucks and more !

ToneZ Features

  • 4 Oscillators
    • 10 waveforms
    • Morphing between waveforms
    • 1-8 voices with detune & stereo width control
    • Free Running / Retrigger & phase control
  • 2 Filter Units
    • 2 routable filters per units, working in serial
    • Low pass, high pass, band pass modes with resonnance & key tracking control
  • 4 Envelopes
    • Routable to oscillator amplitude, pitch or filter cutoff
    • ADSR type
  • 2 LFOs
    • Routable to oscillator amplitude, pitch or filter cutoff
    • 6 wave shapes
  • 6 Built-in Effects
    • Distorsion
    • Equalizer
    • Chorus
    • Delay
    • Reverb
    • Compressor
  • Preset system - share your patches with the world !
  • Poly/Mono legato mode
  • Unison detune feature

ToneZ was made using Cabbage framework. The Csound code is available on Github.
